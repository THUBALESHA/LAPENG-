About Lapeng

Lapeng Aged Care & Support Centre is committed to improving the quality of life of older persons through care, recreation, social engagement, emotional support, and protection from abuse and neglect.


We promote the rights of older persons under South Africa's Older Persons Act and create safe spaces where senior citizens can enjoy meaningful lives, remain active, and feel valued in society.

Our Services

Recreational Activities

Games, exercise programmes, music, arts, crafts, community gatherings and social events.

Emotional Support

Guidance, counselling referrals, companionship and community support for older persons.

Protection Against Abuse

Awareness programmes and support for reporting elder abuse, neglect and exploitation.

Health & Wellness

Wellness education, healthy living programmes and community health awareness initiatives.

Rights of Older Persons

  • ✓ The right to dignity and respect.
  • ✓ Freedom from abuse, neglect and exploitation.
  • ✓ Access to support and care services.
  • ✓ Participation in community activities.
  • ✓ Equal treatment without discrimination.
