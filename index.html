About LAPENG

In every community, our elders are the pillars of wisdom, history, and strength. They are the ones who nurtured us, protected us, and guided us. Now, it is our turn to stand for them

CORE VALUE

At Lapeng Organization, we believe that caring for older persons is not just an act of kindness—it is a responsibility. Many of our elders face loneliness, poverty, health challenges, and neglect. But together, we can change that

MANDATE

South African law is clear. Under the Older Persons Act 13 of 2006, every older person has the right to dignity, protection, proper care, and participation in society. They have the right to live free from abuse—emotional, physical, financial, or otherwise

objevtive

It is our duty to make sure these rights are not just written on paper—but lived, protected, and respected every day
